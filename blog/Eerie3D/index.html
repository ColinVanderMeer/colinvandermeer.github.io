Eerie 3d sci-fi games running on platforms they probably shouldn’t

Published: Sep 13 2023

Welcome back to another post of “Colin screams into the void about things he alone finds interesting.” I've noticed a genre of games (2) that I like to call "Eerie 3d sci-fi games running on platforms they probably shouldn’t." It's an interesting genre born from the choice to use limited hardware and the creativity employed to overcome it.

Automaton Lung

Automaton Lung is a very strange and atmospheric game. The main gameplay loop is walking around a disjointed and surreal world while collecting computer chips. Then moving onto the next world.
